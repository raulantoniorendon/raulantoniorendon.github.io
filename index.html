About
Raul Antonio Rendon Araiza

Raul Antonio Rendon is a Zurich-based director working across commercials, music videos, and short film.

He builds work at the collision point of two unrelated systems. The intersection is where the film lives. Form is never decoration. Every structural choice carries a philosophical argument, and the audience is always a participant in it.

At 21, his films have screened and won across London and Los Angeles, collecting awards for direction and cinematography for Either/Or and KILLHER INSTINCT.

Available for commercial and narrative projects.
